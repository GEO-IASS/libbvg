size_t bitfile_tag::fill

current number of bits in the bit buffer

Remarks:
the bits current & (2 << fill - 1) yield the next bits in the file, where the subsequent bits are exactly: 
   ((current & (1 << fill - 1)) >> (fill - 1)) & 1
   ((current & (1 << fill - 1)) >> (fill - 2)) & 1
   ...
   ((current & (1 << fill - 1)) >> (fill - fill)) & 1
see read_from_current in bitfile.c
